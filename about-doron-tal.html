⯈ Practice
Daily Sitting Meditation Practice Group (2023 - Present)
Daily online group meditations with the Clear Mountain Monastery community and monks.
Ānāpānasati Meditation Method (2020 - Present)
The sixteen-step meditation instructions of the Buddha - an interpretation of the description in Majjhima Nikaya Sutta 118.
Ajahn Brahm's Meditation Method (2019 - 2020)
Followed the description in Ajahn Brahm's book "Mindfulness, Bliss and Beyond".
Walking Meditation (2019 - Present)
Began with Yuttadhammo Bhikkhu's Theravada walking meditation instructions (one note per step), added Bhikkhu Bodhi's three-point technique (picking, placing, shifting) and a six-point method. The approach varies depending on mental state at the start of each session.
Metta Practice (2015 - Present)
Practiced Metta at Tibet House in New York City under the guidance of Rachel Hammerman. Regularly attending Bhikkhu Bodhi's weekly Metta sessions since 2019.
Qigong (1989 - Present)
Learned a Qigong (Chi Kung) set for spine healing, strengthening and stretching, created by Master Jiao Guo Ryu, from Robert Tangora.
Tai Chi (1988 - Present)
Tai Chi is moving meditation. I mainly practice the Yang Style Short Form of Prof. Chen Man Ching and have studied under the instruction of Katherine Hannah, Maggie Newman, Sifu William Dere, and since the late 80's, with Great Grandmaster William C. C. Chen.
Breathwork & Body Scans (Mid 1980's - Present)
Yoga breathing exercises to calm the body via abdominal breathing, counting, and visualization. Also started practicing the body scan.

⯈ Training
Engaged Mindfulness Institute - Mindfulness Teacher Training (May 2024 - April 2025)
The Engaged Mindfulness Institute trains its students to facilitate mindfulness instruction in a trauma-informed manner. The EMI mindfulness teacher training certifies its graduates to teach mindfulness to the general population. It is also one of the teacher training institutions whose certification can be accepted by the International Mindfulness Teachers Association for their Certified Mindfulness Teaching Professional (CMT-P) certification. EMI graduation date: April 7, 2025.
Prison Mindfulness Institute's Path of Freedom - Prison mindfulness teacher/facilitator Training (July - August, 2024)
The Path of Freedom curriculum of the Prison Mindfulness Institute is a mindfulness-based emotional intelligence (MBEI) program developed for incarcerated individuals. It integrates mindfulness practices, emotional intelligence topics and exercises, and cognitive behavioral methods.
MBSR 10-Week Teacher Training Foundations Class (2024)
